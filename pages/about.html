About Me:


Hello! My name is Luthando Nkosi. You probably can tell from my portfolio that I love taking pictures. Haha! While I am not a proffessional photographer,I love to express my art in many creative ways. Makeup being one of the many art forms. I was born a creator! From drawing and painting ,to learning how to do my own makeup, and then learning how to design through tech and sewing my own clothes , I'd say life hasn't been all too boring, but a BLAST!!

I started taking an interest in makeup from when I was a young girl dancing ballet. We had to learn to do our own and each others makeup for performances. My passion started growing again during the Covid-19 pandemic and I started getting comfortable applying more than just lip-gloss and mascara. Since then I've been the family/friend makeup artist and I want to do more than just help my girlies close to me but also be of any help to all the girlies!
